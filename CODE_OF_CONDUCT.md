# Code of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant.

## Our Pledge

We want OpenClawd to be a constructive place for developers, researchers, and contributors working across Solana, AI, security, and open-source infrastructure.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- being respectful in technical disagreement
- sharing actionable feedback
- assuming good intent while staying rigorous about correctness
- helping newer contributors navigate the repo safely

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- doxxing or publishing private information
- abusive language
- deliberately sharing secrets, credentials, or exploit details in public channels before disclosure

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

## Reporting

For conduct issues, contact the maintainers through GitHub. For security-sensitive issues, use the private reporting path in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md).
